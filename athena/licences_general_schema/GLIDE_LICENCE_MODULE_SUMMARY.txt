   ├─ Stores monthly bills
   ├─ Fields: id, bill_number, subscription_id, bill_month, due_date,
   │          amount, paid_amount, balance, status, notes,
   │          is_auto_generated, timestamps, audit fields

   └─ Foreign Key: subscription_id → license_subscriptions(id)

   ├─ Stores payment records

   │          timestamps, audit fields
   └─ Foreign Key: bill_id → bills(id)

⚙️ CRON JOB SETUP
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

REQUIRED CRON JOBS:

1. GENERATE MONTHLY BILLS (1st of each month at midnight)
   0 0 1 * * /usr/bin/curl http://yourdomain.com/cron/generate_monthly_bills/KEY

2. UPDATE OVERDUE BILLS (Daily at 1 AM)
   0 1 * * * /usr/bin/curl http://yourdomain.com/cron/update_overdue_bills/KEY

Note: Replace KEY with your actual cron_secret_key from sch_settings table
